ETF FAQ

ETF analyzer questions

Does a strong ETF score mean it is a recommendation?

No. The score is an educational screening label only.

Why compare expense ratios?

Expense ratios affect long-term ownership costs. The portal displays them for context, not as a ranking recommendation.

Why do SPY and QQQ behave differently?

SPY is broader large-cap exposure, while QQQ is more concentrated in growth and technology-linked holdings.
